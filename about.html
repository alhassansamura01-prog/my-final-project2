About Us

Welcome to Home Rental Service, a trusted housing platform designed to make renting properties easier, faster, and more secure for both property owners and tenants across Sierra Leone.

We are proud to operate in partnership with the Government of Sierra Leone, ensuring that our services meet both professional and national housing standards.

Our company serves as a professional mediator between house owners and tenants. Before any property is listed, we first meet the property owner, inspect the house for quality and standard compliance, and agree on the lowest acceptable rental price. Once verified, the property is then registered on our platform — free of charge.

Property owners can also request registration directly through our website by visiting the Explore page. After your request, our team will visit your property for assessment and registration — no fees required.

Tenants, both local and international, can easily browse and rent verified homes directly from our website — safely, quickly, and conveniently.

Advantages to Property Owners

  • We help secure and protect your property through our government partnership.
  • We protect you from tenant misbehavior and unfair treatment.
  • We make finding tenants easier through our professional platform.
  • We help you generate steady income with verified clients.
  • We provide official digital receipts and rental records to prevent property disputes.
  • You must present valid property documentation before registration.
  • If your property is unfinished, our team can help complete it before listing.

Advantages to Tenants

  • We provide a simple and safe way to rent verified homes.
  • We protect you from landlord misconduct or overcharging.
  • We are trusted and reliable with government partnership and transparency.

Advantages to the Government

  • We help create employment opportunities for young people.
  • We build a national property database for housing and development planning.
  • We support the fight against corruption in the housing sector.
  • We make tax collection easier and more transparent for property owners.

Our Mission

To simplify home renting through innovation, honesty, and exceptional customer service.

Our Vision

To become Sierra Leone’s most reliable and affordable digital platform for rental housing and property management.
